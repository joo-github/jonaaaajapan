Calle Crisologo street in Vigan City

The enchanting street of Calle Crisologo in Vigan City, a gem that absolutely deserves a spot on your itinerary in the Philippines. Located about 400 kilometers north of the country’s capital, Manila City, the historic city of Vigan in the province of Ilocos Sur transports you to the 16th century with its old-world charm and beautifully preserved Spanish colonial architecture. As one of the UNESCO World Heritage Sites in the Philippines, Vigan City is a must-visit, with Calle Crisologo being one of the crown jewels of this historic town in Ilocos Sur province.Vigan City, one of the top historical tourist spots in the Philippines, has masterfully preserved the essence of the Spanish colonial era, making it a unique destination that feels like a step back in time. Don’t miss a sightseeing tour that includes Calle Crisologo with a calesa (horse-drawn carriage) ride, where you’ll feel the history beneath your feet as you stroll along cobblestone streets.
